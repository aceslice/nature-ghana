Discover the Beauty of Ghana

Nature Ghana is a portal that aims to promote and share beauty of nature in Ghana. Currently, it features articles on the diverse natural landscapes Ghana offers from waterfalls in the Ashanti region to savannah in the Northern region.

Why Nature Ghana?

The diverse Natural beauty of Ghana

Ghana is a country with a diverse range of climates, ecosystems and natural landscapes. These include savannahs, wetlands, rainforests, waterfalls and more!
